**Wire** is a Shopify app that lets merchants sell and deliver digital downloads. It's live on the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/wire), rated ★★★★★.

### Merchant use cases
- Inventory management
- Order tracking
- Customizable email delivery

### Customer use case
- Receive an email with the purchase
- Access the digital product instantly
