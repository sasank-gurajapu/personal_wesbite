## Programs

**Product Advocate** — a Customer Success motion for scaling non-enterprise paid teams, covering usage-pattern analysis and upsell campaigns, onboarding email/webinar experiments with A/B testing, ML-based suspension-risk prediction, an expansion-potential scoring system, and webinar cohort/impact analysis.

**Autoflex rollout & improvements** — automated flexible licensing for enterprise expansion, with quarterly billing options, continuous feedback loops, and usage-forecasting built in.

**Enterprise Essentials** — a new pricing tier. Led risk assessment, cannibalization analysis, and pricing-scenario modeling ahead of the [launch](https://blog.postman.com/accelerate-api-first-journey-with-postman-enterprise-solutions/).

**Price elasticity analysis** — enterprise pricing optimization through segmented strategies and revenue-impact modeling.

**Churn projection** — identified at-risk accounts 6 months out, analyzed contributing factors, and stood up early-warning systems.

**Monthly sales review** — standardized recurring pipeline-health reporting with forward-looking projections.

**Industry report** — analyzed Fintech API usage trends.

**Presentation & storyboarding workshop** — trained the team on stakeholder communication and presentation craft.

## Analysis

**Self-serve dashboard for Sales & CS** — an interface for independent report generation and export, covering core KPIs and performance metrics.

**Geography, channel & reseller analysis** — identified market gaps and evaluated reseller feasibility, ROI, and selection criteria.

**Sales rep forecasting analysis** — tracked forecast accuracy, built benchmarking tools, and recommended training/incentive changes.

**Sales manager alerts** — real-time account notifications with customizable triggers, integrated into Slack and email.

**Zendesk lead analysis** — reviewed support tickets for self-serve upsell opportunities and built automated routing plus targeted documentation.

**Webpage notifications for Sales** — in-app notifications and learning alerts with gamification elements.

**Sales funnel analysis** — identified stage bottlenecks and ran A/B tests on conversion-rate improvements.
