## Gems of Maniratnam

One of the few directors in Indian cinema to use Music effectively in movies. Be it incredible background scores and themes to absolute chartbusters. You can trust Maniratnam album to be great.

Of course they would be great, given he has collaborated with 2 of the best composers of the country - Ilayaraja and AR Rahman.

## Kathaga Kalpanaga (Like a story, Like a dream)

Compilation of yesteryear telugu songs which continue to impress me with their lyrical value and hit hard with the emotions like none of the songs in recent times. These are the songs which I cursed for half of my life whenever my parents played them (I'm 25 as I write this), but I've probably become as avid a fan as I grew.

Not many can resist the compositions of Ilayaraja, KV Mahadevan, combined with lyrical genius of Sitarama Sastry and Veturi and vocals of SPB, SP Sailaja and Yesudas. Of course, these are just a few of the stalwarts part of the playlist
